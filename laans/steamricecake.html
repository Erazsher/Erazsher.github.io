STEAMED RICE CAKE RECIPE

Steam rice cake (AKA puttu) is one of the popular cuisine of Kerala. As the name suggest the dish is steam rice cake with grated coconut on the sides. It is comman breakfast of Kerala. It is made using a special utensil natively called a puttu kutti or kudam cooker.

This guide follows recipe for 2 serving

Ingredients

  • flour 1 cup or add as required
  • salt 1/3 teaspoon or add as required
  • grated coconut 1/2cups fresh ones preffered
  • water 2 to 3 cups for steaming
